Godot Engine Features

100% FREE TO USE

Godot is completely free and open-source under the very permissive MIT license. No strings attached, no royalties, nothing. Your game is yours, down to the last line of engine code.

EASY TO USE

Godot provides a huge set of common tools, so you can just focus on making your game without reinventing the wheel.

SCRIPT WITH FULL FREEDOM

More choices than any other engine. (GDScript, Full C# 7.0 support, Full C++ support, Visual scripting, Built-in editor, Integrated documentation.

ANIMATE EVERYTHING

Animate literally everything, from bones and objects to function calls.
custom transition curves and tweens to create incredible animations.

GORGEOUS 3D GRAPHICS

The brand new physically-based renderer comes with a ton of features that will make your games look incredible.

INNOVATIVE DESIGN

Godot comes with hundreds of built-in nodes that make game design a breeze. You can also create your own for custom behaviors, editors and much more.
